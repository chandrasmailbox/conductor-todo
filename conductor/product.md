# Initial Concept
To create a mobile application for tracking personal expenses.

# Product Guide: Mobile Expense Tracker

## 1. Vision
To create an intuitive and user-friendly mobile application that helps students and young adults take control of their personal finances. The application will empower them to track their spending effortlessly and gain a clear understanding of their financial habits.

## 2. Target Audience
The primary users are students and young adults who are managing a tight budget. These users need a simple, straightforward tool to monitor their income and expenses without being overwhelmed by complex features.

## 3. Key Features
The core functionality of the application will focus on:
- **Easy Expense Logging:** Users can quickly add expenses and assign them to predefined or custom categories (e.g., Food, Transport, Entertainment).
- **Visual Reports & Insights:** The application will generate simple, easy-to-understand visual reports (e.g., pie charts, bar graphs) that show where their money is going, helping them identify spending patterns.

## 4. Goals
- **Short-term:** Develop a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) with the key features of expense logging and basic visual reporting.
- **Long-term:** Become a go-to financial companion for young adults, potentially expanding to include features like budget setting, savings goals, and subscription tracking.
